TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS FOR THE DOWNSTREAM



PROJECT EAGLE EYE

The problem of theft, adulteration and diversion has been a disturbing one in the downstream sector of the Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry for years. These problems occur between the point of loading at the bulk storage depot and point of delivery/offload. Eagle eye; an intellect of Pedohis offshore ltd is a total monitoring system for petroleum products in transit from point of loading at the depot to the point of offload at the different locations.

TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS FOR THE DOWNSTREAM



FUEL CONTROL SYSTEM

Fuel control system is a state of the art telematics solution that puts control of stationary and mobile fuel tanks in your hand, allowing you save cost, maintain profitability and meet rising business demands. With this management solution, you can detect leakages due to perforations, faulty hardware and theft while monitoring, auditing and reporting diesel usage trend for accountability purposes.

TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS FOR THE DOWNSTREAM



TIKON VEHICLE TRACKING SOLUTIONS

TIKON is a vehicle tracking solution designed to enhance safety, security and accountability for your fleet I.e petrol trucks and LPG trucks and company vehicles by providing access to real-time data, alerts and reports. This solution caters to all organizations, businesses in the downstream sector of the oil and gas industry and its allied industries. It provides live and instant information regarding the location of your vehicle, updates on any idling and tracks your vehicles when in the possession of a third party. With this information, you can see whenever the third party goes off track and can respond instantly when any issues arise. This state of the art telematics solution has the ability to track up to 5,000 mobile assets.

TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS FOR THE DOWNSTREAM



PRO-DRIVE

Pro-Drive is an In-Vehicle Monitoring System (IVMS) designed to provide cost savings by facilitating effective monitoring and reporting of drivers’ behaviours, performance and vehicle usage based on parameters ranging from Harsh Acceleration, Harsh Braking, Harsh Cornering, Over-speeding and Excessive Idling. Based on the comprehensive profiling and assessment of drivers’ driving habits, scorecards and grades are generated and sent as SMS and Email Notifications to Drivers and Decision Makers serving as a yardstick to either reward or reprimand drivers. This comprehensive driver profiling solution has achieved significant cost savings for businesses and organizations in the oil and gas industry and its allied industries. This technology solution is perfect for organizations looking to enforce safe driving habits, increase the lifespan of their fleet by at least 3 years, get detailed information on the performance of drivers and save millions of Naira annually on Maintenance, Repair and Operations (MRO) costs.
